R5: My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge

1. Why does Laurel Schwulst advocate for individual websites over social media? Do you agree with her? Please explain your answer.


Schwulst advocates for prioritizing individual websites over social media, emphasizing the heightened agency they offer users. A hand-coded website grants full control over the content and its presentation, allowing for greater creative expression compared to the standardized interfaces of social media platforms driven by advertisements. I align with Schwulst's perspective, valuing my personal website as an expansive canvas for self-expression. Through intentional interactions, such as featuring a curated selection of scanned items symbolic of my creative projects, I can provide a more profound insight into my thought process and creative journey. This autonomy in crafting my virtual persona stands in contrast to the limitations imposed by the uniformity of social media interfaces, allowing for endless variations in how I am perceived.
